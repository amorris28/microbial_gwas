Community and geography are correlated and sites cluster around three locations Also, geography uses up more of the similarity matrix space (see histograms) Therefore, I coded geography as three factors (one for each cluster of sites) Also, wetland/upland covaries with those three sites, but moisture content is included in the environmental similarity matrix and should capture that variation
